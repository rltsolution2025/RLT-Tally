About RLT Groups

Established in 1995, RLT Group has grown into a diversified organization with strong expertise in instrumentation, industrial automation, water management solutions and education.

Over the years, RLT Group has built a strong reputation for innovation, quality engineering and reliable project execution across multiple industries.

Through RLT Academy of Eexcellence, the organization focuses on delivering quality education, professional training and industry-oriented skill development programs.

RLT Edzaro – Tally Academy, in partnership with Tally Education, provides practical training in Tally, GST, accounting, payroll and taxation to help students build successful careers in finance and accounting.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Success Story

From a modest beginning to a growing organization empowering thousands through education and innovation.

In the year 1995, a visionary leader, Dr. G. P. Thirumurugan, laid the foundation for what would become the RLT Group. A former Vice President of Larsen & Toubro (L&T) India, Dr. Thirumurugan was not only an accomplished engineer but also a passionate educator.

With just six dedicated individuals, RLT Group began its journey in the fields of instrumentation and water management. These humble beginnings were fueled by determination, integrity, and a commitment to excellence.

As the organization grew, Dr. Thirumurugan turned his attention to education—his true passion. He established schools known for high academic standards and remarkable student success, nurturing young minds and empowering future generations.

Over the years, RLT Group expanded into new domains and executed prestigious government and industrial projects. Today the organization has grown to more than 250+ talented employees.

Expanding into Skill Development – RLT Edzaro

To support future-ready careers, RLT Group launched RLT Edzaro, a platform focused on technology and professional skill development. Through strategic collaborations with industry leaders, Edzaro bridges the gap between academic learning and real-world industry requirements.

RLT Edzaro – Tally Academy

RLT Edzaro – Tally Academy was established as an Authorized Partner of Tally Education. The academy provides industry-relevant training in TallyPrime, GST, Accounting, Payroll, and Taxation.

Students gain hands-on experience through real-time accounting scenarios and practical training delivered by experienced professionals, helping learners build strong careers in finance and accounting.

From a modest beginning with six people to a flourishing organization impacting thousands, the RLT journey continues to grow with a vision of empowering individuals through education, technology, and professional training.
