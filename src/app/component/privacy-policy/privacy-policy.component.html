Privacy Policy

RLT Edzaro Tally Academy

1. Introduction

RLT Edzaro Tally Academy (“the Academy”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is committed to maintaining the highest standards of privacy, confidentiality, and data protection for all individuals who interact with our institution. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal information obtained through our website, training programs, enrollment processes, and communication channels. By accessing our services, you acknowledge and agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Scope of Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of our website, prospective students submitting enquiries, enrolled students participating in our training programs, and individuals engaging with our academic or placement services. It governs all forms of data collection, whether online or offline, and ensures that personal information is handled responsibly and transparently in accordance with applicable regulations and institutional standards.

3. Information We Collect

The Academy collects personal information necessary to provide high-quality educational services. This may include details such as your name, contact number, email address, educational qualifications, and course preferences. In addition, we may collect academic-related information such as course progress, assessments, and certification records to support training and evaluation processes. Technical data, including IP address, browser type, device information, and website interaction patterns, may also be collected automatically to enhance system performance and user experience.

4. Purpose of Data Collection

The information collected is used primarily to facilitate course enrollment, deliver training programs, manage academic records, and communicate important updates related to courses, schedules, and certifications. It also enables us to provide career guidance, placement assistance, and continuous improvement of our educational services. In certain cases, with your consent, we may use your contact information to share relevant updates, announcements, or promotional content related to our programs.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

RLT Edzaro Tally Academy does not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties. However, limited data sharing may occur with trusted service providers, certification bodies, or placement partners strictly for academic or operational purposes and only where necessary. Information may also be disclosed to regulatory authorities or government agencies when required by law or to comply with legal obligations. All such disclosures are carried out with due care to ensure data protection and confidentiality.

6. Data Retention

Personal and academic data may be retained for as long as necessary to fulfill educational, administrative, and legal requirements. Student records, certifications, and training history may be securely stored for future reference, verification, or compliance purposes. Once the data is no longer required, appropriate measures are taken to securely delete or anonymize the information.

7. Data Security

The Academy implements appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal information against unauthorized access, misuse, alteration, or disclosure. These measures include secure servers, controlled access systems, and data protection protocols. While we strive to maintain a secure environment, users are advised that no method of data transmission over the internet is completely secure, and reasonable precautions should be taken when sharing sensitive information.

8. User Rights

Individuals have the right to access, review, and update their personal information maintained by the Academy. Requests for correction, deletion, or withdrawal of consent may be submitted at any time, subject to applicable legal and administrative requirements. We are committed to responding to such requests in a timely and transparent manner.

9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze traffic, and improve functionality. These tools help us understand user behavior and optimize website performance. Users may choose to disable cookies through their browser settings; however, certain features of the website may be affected as a result.

10. Third-Party Links

The Academy’s website may contain links to external websites or third-party platforms for informational or educational purposes. We do not control or take responsibility for the privacy practices of such external sites, and users are encouraged to review their respective privacy policies before sharing any personal information.

11. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in legal requirements, institutional practices, or technological advancements. Any updates will be published on this page with a revised effective date. Continued use of our services after such updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
