Course Overview

A Complete Guide to GST Accounting using TallyPrime

The GST using TallyPrime Program is designed to provide in-depth knowledge of Goods and Services Tax (GST) and its practical implementation using TallyPrime. This course helps learners understand real-time GST accounting processes followed in businesses across India.

This course focuses on GST concepts, registration, tax structure, input tax credit (ITC), GST compliance, and return filing, along with hands-on training in TallyPrime for accurate and efficient tax management.

Through practical sessions, students will gain expertise in GST setup in TallyPrime, recording GST transactions, generating GST invoices, managing purchase and sales with tax, filing GST returns (GSTR-1, GSTR-3B), and analyzing GST reports, preparing them for roles in taxation, accounting, and compliance.

What You Will Learn

Learn the fundamentals of TallyPrime including downloading and installation, launching in education mode, and activating the license. Understand company creation, alteration and deletion, along with pre-defined vouchers. Gain practical knowledge in recording purchase, sales, contra, receipt, payment, and journal entries.

Understand GST concepts, classifications, and place of supply. Learn how to enable GST in TallyPrime, configure GST at group level, and record GST transactions for single and multiple tax slabs. Cover GST services, expenses, assets, reverse charge mechanism (RCM), and enable auto-billing features for efficient GST accounting.

Learn how to customize invoice and voucher printing in TallyPrime. This includes updating invoice numbering, adding company logo, enabling additional stock descriptions, configuring print preferences such as signatures, bank details, jurisdiction, and optimizing paper size. Also learn to enable rate inclusive of GST in invoices.

Learn GST return concepts and types of returns. Understand GST computation and adjustments, GSTR-1 tables, and filing of GSTR-1 including nil returns. Perform GSTR-2 and 2A reconciliation, and learn GSTR-3B filing using both online and offline methods. Also cover GST payments, E-way bill registration, generation, update, and cancellation.

Understand e-invoicing concepts and registration process. Learn enablement, API user creation, and generating e-invoices using TallyPrime. Also cover generation of e-way bill along with e-invoice, Excel utility-based e-invoice generation, and cancellation procedures for complete compliance.
