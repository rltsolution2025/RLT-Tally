Course Overview

A Complete Guide on Advanced Business Accounting and Indian Taxation

The Tally Professional Program is an advanced-level certification designed for learners who already have a basic understanding of financial accounting and want to enhance their expertise in modern accounting practices using TallyPrime.

This program provides a deeper understanding of advanced accounting techniques, taxation, and business finance by combining employability skills with practical training in computerized accounting, finance, and administration.

The course covers inventory management, GST, TDS, TCS transactions, payroll processing, income tax, and financial reporting, with hands-on practical applications using the latest TallyPrime software to ensure learners stay aligned with current industry standards.

Volume 1

Volume 2

Business Transactions & GST Management – Manage purchase & sales, receivables/payables, GST and generate financial reports in TallyPrime.

What You Will Learn

This module covers the introduction to multi-currency, creation and alteration of foreign currencies, defining of the rate of exchange, recording vouchers using foreign currency (On Realization Basis & Un-Realized Basis), adjusting Forex Gain/Loss, and generating reports with multi-currencies.

This module covers the introduction to interest calculation, accounting of simple interest and compound interest, accounting of interest on outstanding balances, and generating the interest calculation reports.

This module covers the introduction to Job Order Processing, accounting of Job Work Out Order processing, accounting of Job Work In Order processing, accounting of transfer of materials (Inter-Godown) and generating Job Work reports.

This module covers the introduction to job costing, features of job costing, types of business that use job costing, documents used in the job costing process, recording job costing related transactions in Tally, and generating job costing reports.

This module covers the introduction to advanced banking transactions, cheque management, post-dated cheque management, and handling e-payments in TallyPrime.

This module covers the overview of Tally.NET features such as Remote Access, TallyPrime Reports in Browsers (TRiB), Data Synchronisation, and Tally Virtual User (TVU).

This module covers the introduction to E-Way Bill, GST e-Invoicing, Recording GST compliant transactions such as Intrastate Supply of Goods & Services, Interstate Supply of Goods, Purchases from Unregistered Dealer, Purchases from Composition Dealer, Accounting Mixed Supply and Composite Supply, Supply of Exempted/Nil-rated Goods, Supply of Goods to Special Economic Zone (SEZ), Supply of Goods to Other Territory, Supply of Goods to Unregistered Dealer, Reverse Charge Mechanism under GST, Export of Goods, Advance Receipt, Generating E-Invoice Report, Generating E-Way Bill Report, Input Tax Credit Set Off against Liability, GST Tax Payment, Reversal of Input Tax Credit, Generating and E-Filing GST Returns in Tally.

This module covers the introduction to Tax Deducted at Source, Recording TDS Compliant Transactions such as Booking of Expenses & deducting TDS in the Journal Voucher, TDS Transaction for Purchase of Software, TDS on Sales Commission and Deducting TDS, Accounting TDS on Capital Expenditure, Accounting for TDS on Advance Payments and Adjusting Advances towards Bill, Purchase Escalation after Recording TDS Transaction, Reversal/Cancellation of Expenses with TDS, TDS on Works Contract attracting GST, TDS on Payments to Non-Residents (DTAA), TDS on Income from Foreign Currency, Bonds or Shares of India Company, Accounting Direct Payment and deducting TDS, Accounting Multiple Expenses and Deducting TDS later, TDS on Reimbursement of Expenses (Pure Agent), TDS Payment to Department, generating and E-Filing Form 26Q and Form 27Q, TRACES Portal and Maintaining Books of Accounts as a Deductee.
