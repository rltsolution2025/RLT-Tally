Course Overview

A Complete Guide on Business Accounting

This entry-level certification helps learners become proficient in computerized accounting using Tally. The TallyEssential certification is designed for beginners in the Accounting and Finance domain to understand financial accounting concepts from fundamentals to advanced levels.

The program is structured into three learning stages: Level 1, Level 2 and Level 3, together forming the Tally Comprehensive Certification.

Tally Essential Comprehensive is a complete course on computerized accounting using TallyPrime. It includes all modules from Tally Essential Levels 1–3 and provides hands-on practical learning aligned with the latest TallyPrime software updates.

Level 1

Recording and Reporting – Learn accounting fundamentals, company creation, ledger management and voucher entries in TallyPrime.

Level 2

Accounts Payable and Receivable – Manage inventory, purchase and sales entries, GST basics and financial reports in TallyPrime.

Level 3

Taxation and Compliance – Understand GST transactions, tax calculations, compliance requirements and advanced financial reporting.

Tally Comprehensive

Complete certification covering Level 1, Level 2 and Level 3 with practical training using the latest TallyPrime software.

What You Will Learn

This module summarizes the Fundamentals of Accountancy, starting with the accounting terms, concepts, right up to the double entry system of accounting. It explains the need and usage of Golden Rules of Accounting in the books of accounting and recording the business transactions.

This introductory module on Accounting using Tally explains how simple it is to download and install Tally in the system. This module helps you understand the simplest way of creating a company and the company features and configurations.

This module shows you how simple it is to create, alter and display the Chart of Accounts such as Ledgers, Groups, Stock Items and Units of Measure Creation.

This module covers how to record transactions such as Receipt, Contra, Payment, Purchase, Sales, Debit Note, Credit Note and Journal Vouchers with practical scenarios.

This module covers banking features including cheque printing, bank reconciliation and generating deposit slips.

Learn to generate financial reports such as Trial Balance, Balance Sheet, Profit and Loss Account, Cash Flow Statements and Inventory Reports.

This module covers securing financial information, creating user roles, security control and managing system users.

Learn how to backup, restore and manage company data securely without any loss.

Introduction to GST in TallyPrime including GST transactions, Input Tax Credit set-off, GST payment and GST return generation.
