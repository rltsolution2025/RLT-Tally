Course Overview

A Complete Guide to Payroll & Income Tax using TallyPrime

This course provides in-depth knowledge of payroll processing and income tax management using TallyPrime, a simple and flexible accounting software widely used in the industry. The Payroll & Income Tax Program is designed to help learners understand real-time payroll systems, salary processing, and tax calculations.

Through practical, real-world scenarios, learners will gain expertise in handling statutory deductions such as Provident Fund (PF), Employee State Insurance (ESI), Professional Tax, and Tax Deducted at Source (TDS), along with generating accurate payroll reports and payslips.

The program focuses on hands-on training to manage complex payroll transactions, ensure compliance with government regulations, and develop job-ready skills required for careers in payroll, HR, and taxation.
